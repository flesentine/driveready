DriveReady CA DMV Prep

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

DriveReady CA DMV Prep is a California DMV study app. The app is built to help users study road signs, answer practice questions, review missed answers, and prepare for the permit test.

Information We Do Not Collect

DriveReady does not require account creation. We do not sell personal information. We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, health information, or advertising identifiers.

Study Progress

The app may store study progress, practice results, missed answers, preferences, and Pro Pass entitlement status locally on your device so the app can remember your progress and unlocked features.

Purchases

Pro Pass purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. DriveReady does not receive or store your payment card information. Restore Purchases uses Apple App Store purchase records to restore eligible purchases.

Support Contact

If you contact support by email, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include so we can respond to your request.

Children

DriveReady is a general DMV study app and is not designed to collect personal information from children.
